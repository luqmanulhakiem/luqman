About Project

I spent my Jan - Juni 2024 at Bangkit Academy. When I was a Mobile Developer cohort at Bangkit Academy (Google, Goto, Traveloka), I had an extraordinary opportunity to learn and create various projects related to Artificial Intelligence (AI). I even got the opportunity to lead a team in working on a final project (capstone project). My team decided to take the idea of ​​creating a booking application for booking trips to the Indonesian mountains. We got this idea because of the rampant fraud and fraud committed by parties organizing trips. With us as a 3rd party acting as a bridge, we hope to be able to solve the problems experienced by climbers, both beginners and experienced.

GETTING STARTED
Understanding the Current HighKing App

The Highking App(not launched) is a trip booking platform for mountain hiking in Indonesia, designed to make your journey easier and more enjoyable. With intelligent recommendation features based on personal preferences, Highking helps you discover hiking destinations that match your desires, skill level, and available time. Explore various mountains across Indonesia, schedule your trips, and enjoy a safe and well-organized hiking experience with trusted trip organizers.

The Problem

we found The rise of fraud cases by trip organizers in mountain hiking has caused significant losses, especially for beginner hikers.

How Can We Solve The Problem ?
  • Provide reliable information
  • Ensure mountaineers safety
  • secure payment
  • Streamline booking process
